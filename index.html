Advancing Surgery Through Ethical AI

At the ARGOS Lab, we integrate computer vision, machine learning, and clinical expertise to develop AI that enhances surgical precision while prioritizing responsibility, generalizability, and openness. We ask:

How can AI empower surgeons without compromising patient trust?

Our Research Methodology

Computer Vision for Surgery

Leveraging computer vision, we analyze surgical videos for real-time instrument tracking and phase recognition, validated at the University Medical Center.

Diverse Surgical Datasets

Our federated learning datasets ensure AI generalizes across diverse hospitals and patient demographics.

Interdisciplinary Collaboration

We collaborate with surgeons and ethicists to ensure AI aligns with clinical ethics and enhances human expertise.
